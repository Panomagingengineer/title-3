recommendation

Based on the interviews, observation, and conclusion of the study, the following recommendations are offered:
1. Further experimental study should be conducted to determine the shelf life of the edible straw made from mango skin, banana peel, and pineapple rind.
2. The taste of the edible straw must be improved to be acceptable to the teenager as well as to the kids.
3. Future researchers must conduct other experimental studies using other fruit peels.
