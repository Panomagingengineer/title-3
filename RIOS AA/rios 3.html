Methodology

This experimental, quantitative study examined the effect of Aloe vera extract on the growth of red onions grown hydroponically. The independent variable was the concentration of Aloe vera extract, while dependent variables included leaf and bulb color, number of leaves, and root length. Controlled variables included onion type, light, temperature, and hydroponic setup.
Six hydroponic systems were prepared, each with 15 oz of water and varying Aloe vera concentrations.
